Hello, world!

My name is Scott and I'm a self-taught developer.

I initially began my career in sales. Oddly, my colleagues always remarked that I seemed more like a programmer than a salesman. This came as a surprise to me since I thought it was normal for salespeople to:

  • convert sales presentations into flowchart form with if-then statements to make it easier to teach the pitch to new hires
  • track sales funnels in Excel to identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies
  • Google everything they don't understand
I've since devoted most of my free time to learning the fundamentals of computer science in order to change my career trajectory.
